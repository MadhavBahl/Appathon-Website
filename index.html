FAQ

All the important questions, Answered



Who all can participate?

Students irrespective of their college/branch can take part in this event.

What about food and beverages?

Its all on us! We would provide you with everything you need. We need you to concentrate on the code.


Is it an individual or team-challenge?

Both are fine by us, but in case you have team it can have a maximum of 3 participants.

Do we need to have the entire idea fully functional?

The entire idea need not be fully implemented. However, the submission should be functional so that it can be reviewed by the judges.


What's the registration fee?

The registration fee is 100 rupees only.

Who will own the IP(Intellectual Property) and rights to the product that I have built?

The developer/developers of the web/mobile application will have all rights and own the IP of the product. However, all code needs to be all public domain (open source) so that it can be evaluated by the judges.


What are the skill required?

Both are fine by us, but in case you have team it can have a maximum of 3 participants.

Do we need to have the entire idea fully functional?

Whether you are a coder,designer or a creative tech enthusiast you are welcome to the event. Even if you don't know coding we will be having technical sessions before the event and mentors during the event.
